Future Continuous Tense

Second Conditional

Pengertian: Second Conditional digunakan untuk menyatakan situasi yang tidak nyata atau hipotetis di masa kini atau masa depan.

Pola Kalimat:

  • If + Subject + Verb(Past), Subject + would + Verb(Base).

Contoh Kalimat:

If I had a car, I would drive to work.

If she were rich, she would travel the world.

If they studied harder, they would pass the exam.
