Simple Past Tense

Pengertian: Simple Past Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang terjadi dan selesai di masa lampau.

Pola Kalimat:

  • Positive: Subject + Verb(Past)
  • Negative: Subject + did not + Verb(Base)
  • Question: Did + Subject + Verb(Base)?

Contoh Kalimat:

I visited my grandparents yesterday.

She did not go to the park last week.

Did they play soccer last Sunday?
