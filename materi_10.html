Future Continuous Tense

Modal Verbs

Pengertian: Modal Verbs adalah kata kerja bantu yang memberikan makna tambahan, seperti kemungkinan, izin, atau keharusan.

Beberapa Modal:

  • Can: Kemampuan atau izin.
  • May: Kemungkinan atau izin formal.
  • Must: Keharusan.
  • Will: Janji atau prediksi masa depan.
  • Should: Saran atau kewajiban ringan.

Contoh Kalimat:

She can play the piano very well.

You may leave early today.

We must finish the project by tomorrow.

He will call you later.

You should eat more vegetables.
