Future Continuous Tense

Pengertian:

Future Continuous Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang akan berlangsung pada waktu tertentu di masa depan.

Pola Kalimat:

  • Positive: Subject + will be + Verb(-ing)
  • Negative: Subject + will not be + Verb(-ing)
  • Question: Will + Subject + be + Verb(-ing)?

Contoh Kalimat:

Positive: She will be traveling to France next week.

Negative: We will not be attending the meeting tomorrow.

Question: Will they be playing football this afternoon?
