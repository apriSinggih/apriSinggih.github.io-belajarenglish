Present Simple Tense

Pengertian: Present Simple Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan fakta umum, kebiasaan, atau jadwal rutin.

Pola Kalimat:

  • Positive: Subject + Verb(Base) / Verb+s (untuk subjek tunggal)
  • Negative: Subject + do/does + not + Verb(Base)
  • Question: Do/Does + Subject + Verb(Base)?

Contoh Kalimat:

She reads books every night.

We do not eat fast food often.

Does he work on weekends?
