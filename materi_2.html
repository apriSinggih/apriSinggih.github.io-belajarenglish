Present Continuous Tense

Pengertian:

Present Continuous Tense digunakan untuk:

  • Menyatakan sesuatu yang sedang terjadi saat ini.
  • Menyatakan rencana di masa depan.

Pola Kalimat:

  • Positive: Subject + is/am/are + Verb(-ing)
  • Negative: Subject + is/am/are + not + Verb(-ing)
  • Question: Is/Am/Are + Subject + Verb(-ing)?

Contoh Kalimat:

(Saat ini): He is watching TV right now.

(Rencana): I am meeting my friend tomorrow.

Are you coming to the party tonight?
