Present Perfect Tense

Pengertian: Present Perfect Tense digunakan untuk menyatakan aktivitas yang sudah selesai tetapi memiliki hubungan dengan waktu sekarang.

Pola Kalimat:

  • Positive: Subject + have/has + Verb(Past Participle)
  • Negative: Subject + have/has + not + Verb(Past Participle)
  • Question: Have/Has + Subject + Verb(Past Participle)?

Contoh Kalimat:

I have finished my homework.

She has not visited her grandparents yet.

Have they ever been to Paris?
