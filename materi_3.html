Subject-Verb Agreement

Pengertian: Subject-Verb Agreement adalah aturan kecocokan antara subjek dan kata kerja berdasarkan jumlah (singular/plural).

Aturan Dasar:

  • Subjek Tunggal: Gunakan kata kerja singular (is, has, verb+s).
  • Subjek Jamak: Gunakan kata kerja plural (are, have, verb).

Contoh Kalimat:

The book is on the table. (Singular)

The books are on the table. (Plural)

She writes a letter every week.

They write letters every week.
